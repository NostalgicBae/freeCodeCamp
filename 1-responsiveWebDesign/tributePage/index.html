Michael Dell

Founder of the biggest PC manufacturing company worlwide

  • 1965 - Born in Houston, Texas.
  • 1984 - Founded "PCs Limited" while renrolled at the University of Texas, he later dropped out of college to persue his venture full-time.
  • 1988 - "PCs Limited" was renamed "Dell Inc." and went public.
  • 1992 - Michael Dell becomes the youngest CEO in history to have a company enter the Fortune top 500 corporations.
  • 1999 - Dell releases a book called "Direct from Dell: Strategies That Revolutionized an Industry", where he outline the story of his company.
  • 2004 - Dell steps down as CEO but remains chariman of the board.
  • 2005 - The "Michael & Susan Dell Foundation" donated millions of dollars to help victims of the 2004 tsunami in southern Asia.
  • 2007 - Following a major crisis, Dell regained his role as CEO to oversee "Dell 2.0"
  • 2016 - Dell acquired EMC (a computer-storage coporation) fro $67B, making it the largest technology company acquisition ever recorded.
